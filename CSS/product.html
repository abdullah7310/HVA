Bolt Earbuds

The Bolt Earbud is a high-performance, wireless audio device offering superior sound quality, Bluetooth connectivity, and ergonomic design for comfortable wear. Features include noise cancellation, touch controls, long battery life, and a compact charging case. Sweat and water-resistant, they are ideal for active lifestyles, providing a sleek and stylish audio solution for music, calls, and workouts.

  • Superior sound quality
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Ergonomic design
  • Noise cancellation
  • Touch controls
  • Long battery life
  • Compact charging case
  • Sweat and water-resistant
